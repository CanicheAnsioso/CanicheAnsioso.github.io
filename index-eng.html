Cani-Ya Webpage

Cani-Ya it's an app created and directed for Argentine's employment known as diareros y canillitas, people who sell and distribute national and international newspapers, periodicals and magazines. It's idea behind is to facilitate the management of what corresponds to a (local) newspaper kiosk through of an easy-to-use platform.

My job here was to give life to the project website, both in design and it's development in HTML, CSS and JS, with the necessary information for it's download and composition; given that it corresponds to a very varied range in age and gender.

Web Design & Development

Real State Project

Real Estate is a project of assiduous design and in-depth development; from the mock-up to it's built in HTML, CSS and JS. Assembled from a place of practice and learning, this is a project with ambition and affection for the UI-UX.

It is fully responsive for different devices although centrally detailed for the web. It has detailed search filters, passage to a contact and personalized attention from the real estate house, offers and outstanding properties, illustrations and animations. The color palette was chosen from a soft but luxurious thought, eye-catching but sophisticated, of well-being and tranquility, of wealth and a homely feeling.

App Design

BakeBook

BakeBook is a fictitious app project whose purpose is to create a pleasant environment where to upload, share and see recipes within the gastronomic environment of pastry, bakery and professional and homely patisserie.

It has a specialized search filter to find specific recipes for both keywords such as by likes or intolerances. It also allows commenting and liking the publications of other people.

App Design

ReadySetSports!

ReadySetSports is a fictitious app project whose functionality is to organize the sports activities of those who use it, in addition to notifying you about sporting events in the area.

It allows you to order your days, your month, your schedules according to the activities you have daily. With an alarm system, ReadySetSports will remind you to hydrate, eat healthy, exercise, or rest.
